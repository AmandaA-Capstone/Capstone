Introduction

This project aims to provide a readily available tool for proactive health management, catering to the needs of the elderly, athletes, and the general public. With a focus on real-time health monitoring, user-friendly interface, and personalized prediction, this smartwatch empowers individuals to take control of their well-being.

Objectives

  • Real-time health monitoring
  • User-friendly interface
  • Low cost
  • Personalized prediction to help users take control of their health

Features

  • Heart Rate Monitoring
  • Blood Oxygen Concentration
  • Accelerometer
  • Gyroscope
